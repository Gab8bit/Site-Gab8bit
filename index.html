BIO







I am a young Italian guy who has loved computer science since he was a kid. I try to build my future doing what I love.

I like to make PC programs, Android apps and websites. Thanks to coding, I have learned to develop Discord bots.

Sometimes I spend time modding android devices.

Recently I got into cybersecurity and still enjoy using some hacks to learn new things.








I have worked for some small theater productions as an audio and video technician.

I also do audio and video editing for myself and third parties.

I consider this the best part, because there is a lot of fun to be had during the events.








I started taking photos with a simple smartphone, and then upgraded to a quality camera (a Nikon D5100).

Photography has always been a "hidden hobby" for me, I enjoy and relax at the same time.

I have taken some shots at some weddings, baptisms, birthdays and private events.
